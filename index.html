Niigata University Short Stay Visit Program for international students.



The Niigata University Short Stay Visit Program (SSVP) invites students from Niigata University's partner institutions to experience not only the academic environment at the dental faculty, but also a variety of cultural and social events that help students feel welcome on our campus.

SSVP students stay at the university's residential facilities or in accommodations near to the campus and will able to take seminar-classes alongside other international students and participate in a variety of extracurricular programs and events that are open to the community.

We are eager to learn about the unique insights visiting students bring from their universities, and we are happy to incorporate them in our academic community.
